How to register

01) Enter your Badge number correctly.

02) Enter your NIC number in correct format

03) Select your position

04) Enter your first name and last name.

05) Enter your Phone number (in 10 digits)

06) Enter your email address in required format

07) Enter your password (password must be at least 6 caracters long)

08) Submit your registration details

Note: you can not create your account until you accept the privacy and policy
