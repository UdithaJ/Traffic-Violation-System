Updated Traffic Fine List

A gazette notice for increasing has issued on 20th of July by the Ministry of Transport.They indicates that violations of traffic laws directly cause for many road accidents.Therefore the minimum fine affected on 4 traffic offenses was increased up to Rs.20,000 and updated another fines for some categories.

  1. Driving without a license

    • Rs.20,000

  2. Driving without an insurance cover

    • Rs.20,000 - Rs.30,000

  3. Driving under the influence of alcohol

    • Rs.20,000 - Rs.50,000

  4. High Speeding

    • Rs.2000 - Rs.3000
    • Rs.3000 - Rs.5000

  5. Overtaking from the left side

    • Rs.1500 - Rs.3000
    • Rs.3000 - Rs.4000
    • Rs.4000 - Rs.5500

  6. In case of death

    • Cancellation of the driving license
    • Imprisonment for life
